AJax Arras - Credits

AJax Arras (formerly named AJPlayz7's Arras) was started in 2019 thanks to the help of EletrixScratchCat and AurumMighty, using the original arras template. Despite various breaks and life in general slowing development down, the server persists today and is beyond my wildest visions for it. 6 years ago a couple self-taught strangers came together to make a random tank, the Velveter, and here we are now. Thank you everyone.
